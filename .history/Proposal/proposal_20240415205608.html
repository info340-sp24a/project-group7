Project Proposal: Plant Parenthood

Current Problem

The domain we’re interested in exploring is plant care and gardening, specifically houseplants. Many people, especially beginners, struggle with keeping their plants alive and healthy due to a lack of knowledge about proper care routines, watering schedules, sunlight/soil requirements, etc. Houseplant education is important for reasons such as improving air quality, stress reduction and sustainable living. However, the lack of plant education often leads to plants dying unnecessarily, which not only wastes resources but also diminishes the potential benefits they could provide. Many people may purchase houseplants without fully understanding their specific requirements, leading to improper watering, inadequate light exposure and neglect. This lack of knowledge can result in frustration and discouragement for individuals attempting to incorporate greenery into their homes.

The information technology in this space is the need for an accessible and user-friendly platform that offers users with personalized plant care guidance tailored for their specific plant. This website could provide general information, personalized recommendations and reminders for watering, sunlight, diagnosing or other important tasks tailored for each user’s specific plants. How can we effectively educate individuals on houseplants and how to care for different species in a simple, accessible and user-friendly interface?

The internet is overflowing with plant care advice, but sifting through endless websites and confusing apps can be overwhelming. Many popular options lock essential features behind paywalls or worse, bombard you with shopping carts disguised as helpful guides. We believe plant care should be accessible and straightforward. That's why we're building a completely free application, focused solely on nurturing your leafy companions. No in-app purchases, no irrelevant clutter, just pure plant parenthood support!

Proposed Application

Our app is a plant-care website that aims to educate, inform, and remind people about the importance of learning how to care for plants in their homes and guide them through that process with interactive features. We hope to assist beginners through their first steps and help experienced users freshen up their existing knowledge. The app caters to anyone interested in improving their living environment with houseplants, whether they're seeking the aesthetic appeal or the well-documented air quality and stress reduction benefits.

With our app, users can:

  • Search & Filter: Provide users with the option to maintain digital plant care diaries, where they can record observations, photos, and notes about their plants' growth, development, and experiences over time.This allows users to track the improvement/changes that the plant has had through visually. Provide users with the ability to upload photos of their plants at various stages of growth and health. This could include photos taken before and after watering, as well as images showing any signs of distress or improvement. Users can annotate these photos with notes or comments to document their observations.Create a visual gallery within the app where users can view all the photos they've uploaded for each plant. This gallery could display thumbnails of the photos along with timestamps, allowing users to track the progress of their plants over time and identify any patterns or trends.
  • Search & Filter: Users can search for specific plants by name or filter by categories like light needs, watering requirements, or species.
  • Learn With Flashcards: With flashcards, users can test their knowledge of the different types of plants. Users can interact with these flashcards by clicking on them. The flashcards have one interactive feature, which would flip from questions to answers when users interact with it. A progress tracking system can motivate users and measure their learning journey.
  • Stay on Track With a Calender: Users can create profiles for their plants, inputting details like plant type, pot size, and acquisition date. The app then generates personalized care schedules with reminders for watering, fertilizing, and repotting based on the specific plant's needs and the user's environment.
  • Access Detailed Biographies: Access detailed and engaging biographies for various plant types, including historical background, interesting facts, and uses.

We want to utilize existing data to make the process of gathering plant information more efficient. The Perunual Plant API provides information on a variety of houseplants, garden plants, trees, and flowers. Users can search for information by species name, common name, or other keywords. The API provides details including botanical data, care guides, and images.

Why Our Solution?

Our app can be a great solution as it removes the financial barrier to plant knowledge and presents clear, concise information in a single location. Personalized care plans based on user input ensure proper watering, sunlight, and other needs for each specific plant. Reminders and actionable guidance empower users to take the right steps at the right time, promoting plant survival and growth. Interactive features like flashcards further engage users and solidify their understanding of plant care. This combination of clear information, personalized plans, and interactive features empowers users to confidently navigate plant parenthood, ultimately leading to healthier plants.
